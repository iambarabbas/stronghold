What's Broken

The core product is exceptional. The delivery system is not. These structural gaps are actively eroding a strong foundation and represent over $500K in annual recovery opportunity.

Key Finding

Retention is an engagement and relationship problem, not an affordability problem. Donors giving $15/month churn at roughly the same rate as donors giving $40/month. Investing in communication quality and impact reporting will do more for retention than adjusting ask amounts.

The Problem

The right pages exist on the site: emergency medicine, humanitarian relief, refugee protection. They are well-linked internally.

But they don't rank because Google receives no external signal about what Stronghold is. Of all inbound link anchors:

  • 90.4% are brand name variations
  • Only 3.5% contain topical terms

Google knows Stronghold exists. It does not know what Stronghold does.

Peer Comparison

Stronghold shares zero keywords with comparable organizations:

  • Project Dynamo
  • Free Burma Rangers
  • Team Rubicon

These organizations rank for the searches that should lead to Stronghold. We are simply not in the same search conversation.

The YouTube Conversion Gap

YouTube sends 48x more traffic than Facebook to the website but converts at 0%. This is the single largest untapped opportunity.

Channel Traffic Conversions
YouTube 34,381 views 0
Facebook 710 views 7

YouTube visitors browse content pages (Media, Newsletter, FAQ) and leave. There is no conversion path designed for video-referred visitors.

What's Missing

No landing page that says: "You just watched this. Here's how to help."

No intent-matched donation ask for video audiences.

Designing even a basic YouTube-to-donor funnel could unlock substantial acquisition volume from traffic that already exists at zero media cost.

Attribution Blindness

We cannot tell which channels, campaigns, or content pieces are driving donor acquisition. Strategic decisions are being made without visibility.

90% Unattributed

Approximately 90% of donations lack proper attribution data. UTM tracking is not implemented across traffic sources.

Broken Tracking

One-time donation tracking has never fired in GA4. 50-70% of donation activity is invisible in analytics.

No CRM

No system exists for tracking donor intent, journey progression, or lifecycle analytics.

Why This Matters

Without attribution, it is impossible to calculate channel-level ROI, identify which podcast appearances drove the most sign-ups, or make informed decisions about where to invest marketing resources. We are flying blind.
