Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about supporting Stronghold Rescue & Relief.

How can I get regular updates on Stronghold's work?

Stronghold sends out a monthly newsletter to all of our email subscribers with updates from the field, impact reports, and stories of the families we've helped. You can sign up here.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

Yes! Stronghold Rescue & Relief is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit organization. Your donation is 100% tax-deductible to the extent allowed by law. Our EIN is 83-2711117.

Can I send physical checks to Stronghold?

Yes. You can mail checks to:

Stronghold Rescue & Relief
P.O. Box 493
Menomonee Falls, WI 53052

Thank you!

Can I send physical items to be donated?

We appreciate the gesture, but please do not send physical items. We source all of our needs directly from the communities where we work — this keeps costs low and ensures we get exactly what's needed. We recommend donating physical goods to charities in your local community.

How do I get my year-end giving statement for taxes?

Stronghold sends out year-end giving statements every January to the email address you provided when signing up online. If you sent a physical check, we mail your statement to the address on the check. If you didn't receive your giving statement, please contact us and we'll get it to you right away.

Can I pause or adjust my monthly donation?

Yes! You can log in to your giving account using the receipt you received when signing up. Once signed in, you can pause, cancel, or adjust your donation amount at any time.

Does Stronghold accept volunteers?

We do not accept volunteers except in special circumstances where a particular skillset is required. Our operations take place in extremely dangerous conflict zones, and for safety reasons, we only deploy trained combat veterans with specific experience.

Do I get the free t-shirt with my monthly donation?

Yes! When you sign up as a monthly supporter at $15/month or more, we'll send you a free Stronghold t-shirt. Shipping is available to the US and Canada only. Make sure to include your shirt size and mailing address when you sign up!

What is "Charity with Dignity"?

"Charity with Dignity" is our philosophy of empowerment over dependency. Rather than simply delivering aid, we train and enable local teams to conduct their own rescue and relief operations. This approach means your support goes 10× further because we're building lasting capacity in communities that can help themselves long after we leave.
