About Stronghold

A veteran-led nonprofit on a mission to protect the most vulnerable people in the world's most dangerous places.

Our Mission

We send small teams of veterans into conflict zones to protect and care for persecuted communities — providing emergency medicine, rescuing targeted families, and delivering humanitarian relief.

Our Vision

A world where no family faces persecution alone. We envision communities equipped, trained, and empowered to protect themselves — with Stronghold by their side when they need us most.

How It Started

Stronghold Rescue & Relief was founded by veterans who witnessed firsthand the atrocities faced by civilian populations in conflict zones. After years of military service, they couldn't stand by while families were slaughtered, enslaved, and persecuted.

Starting with nothing but their training and a commitment to help, these veterans began conducting rescue operations and providing medical care in some of the world's most dangerous places. What started as a small team has grown into a full-time operation serving multiple countries.

Today, Stronghold's staff is comprised entirely of combat veterans — most from the special operations community including U.S. Navy SEALs and U.S. Army Ranger Regiment. Their unique skills and experience allow them to operate in environments too dangerous for traditional humanitarian organizations.

Our Philosophy

Charity With Dignity

We believe the best way to create lasting change is to empower communities to help themselves. That's why we don't just deliver aid — we train and enable local teams to conduct their own rescue and relief operations.

This approach means your support goes 10× further. Instead of creating dependency, we're building capacity that lasts long after our teams leave.

What This Means in Practice

  • Local medics trained to provide trauma care
  • Communities equipped with early warning systems
  • Sustainable infrastructure built to last
  • Local partners who can train others

Our Values

Compassion

We see every person as worthy of protection and dignity, regardless of their circumstances or background.

Courage

We go where others can't or won't, facing danger to protect those who cannot protect themselves.

Empowerment

We believe in building capacity, not dependency — training others to carry the mission forward.

Committed to Transparency

We believe donors deserve to know exactly how their support is being used. That's why we make our financial records publicly available.
