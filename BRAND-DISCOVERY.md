### Mission & Positioning
**Core Message:** "We send small teams of veterans into conflict zones to protect and care for persecuted communities."

**Key Differentiator:** "Charity With Dignity" — organizing, training, and enabling teams of locals to provide medical care and conduct their own rescue and relief operations with minimal outside help. Claim: "Your support goes 10X as far."

**Team Composition:** All full-time rescue and relief staff are combat veterans, mostly from special operations community (U.S. Navy SEALs, U.S. Army Ranger Regiment).

## Service Areas (3 Pillars)

### 1. Emergency Medicine
- Direct medical care near point of injury (frontline work)
- Casualty evacuations to definitive care
- Training and mentorship (basic bleeding control to TCCC certification)
- Operate a "Boat Ambulance" for medical evacuations

### 2. Refugee Protection
- Covert extractions of targeted families (from Taliban, cartels, socialist regimes, etc.)
- Security escort for fleeing refugees
- Early warning communications networks in areas with no comms
- Active in Burma (Myanmar) — footage shows Burma Army conflicts

### 3. Humanitarian Relief
- Short-term emergency relief (water, food, shelter, clothing)
- Long-term development projects for refugee camps and remote villages

## Preliminary Observations

### Strengths
1. **Clear differentiation** — "Charity With Dignity" + veteran operators is compelling
2. **Tangible work** — Boat ambulance, real operations, video content
3. **Low barrier to entry** — $0.50/day is accessible
4. **Transparency** — 990 forms available, FAQ addresses common questions
5. **Active content** — Monthly newsletter, YouTube videos

### Concerns/Opportunities
1. **Broken links** — Media page 404, navigation inconsistencies
2. **Social fragmentation** — TikTok linked but dead, low X engagement
3. **Footer missing social links** — Only visible on error pages
4. **No clear impact metrics on homepage** — "By the Numbers" exists in newsletter but not prominently featured
5. **Limited SEO/discovery** — Small social footprint
6. **Volunteer policy** — "We don't accept volunteers" might discourage engagement
